I Am

Poonam Mahajan

Mother, Member of Parliament, Voice for all living beings
Poonam Mahajan, born on December 9, 1980, is an Indian politician representing the Mumbai North Central Constituency in the Lok Sabha, Maharashtra, for the Bharatiya Janata Party (BJP). She served as the National President of Bharatiya Janata Yuva Morcha (BJYM) from 2016 to 2020. Mahajan, the daughter of late BJP leader Pramod Mahajan, entered politics in 2006 and has since held various organizational positions within the party. She gained national prominence by winning the Lok Sabha seat from Mumbai North Central in 2014 and again in 2019.

Mahajan is actively involved in animal welfare, environmental conservation, and youth empowerment initiatives. She played a significant role in organizing events like the Global Citizen Festival India and the Elephant Parade India. Additionally, she has introduced several private member's bills focusing on various social issues during her tenure as a parliamentarian. Mahajan is married to Anand Rao and has two children.
