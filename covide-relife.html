The Unseen Warriors

The Unseen Warriors In the midst of the COVID-19 pandemic, Poonam Mahajan emerged as a beacon of hope. She spearheaded a relief initiative that provided essential supplies to the most vulnerable. The initiative reached out to thousands of families, providing them with food, masks, and sanitizers. The relief work was not just about providing supplies; it was about giving people hope and strength to fight the pandemic.

A Helping Hand

A Helping Hand When the second wave of COVID-19 hit, Poonam Mahajan was quick to respond. She set up a COVID relief fund to help those affected by the pandemic. The fund was used to provide financial assistance to families who had lost their income due to the pandemic. It also helped in setting up temporary COVID care centers to treat the infected. Poonam’s swift action and dedication were a lifeline for many during these challenging times.

The Power of Community

The Power of Community Poonam Mahajan believed in the power of community. She mobilized local communities to participate in the relief work. From distributing supplies to creating awareness about the virus, the community members played a crucial role in the relief work. Poonam’s efforts not only provided immediate relief but also strengthened the community spirit. Her work is a testament to the fact that when communities come together, they can overcome any challenge.
